Rethinking Bloom's Taxonomy for the AI Age

As AI becomes more capable at lower-order thinking, we must redesign education.

AI Excels at Lower-Order Thinking

Remember: Memorizing facts (ChatGPT does this instantly)

Understand: Explaining concepts (AI provides accurate explanations)

Apply: Solving routine problems (AI executes procedures)

The Problem: Traditional education focused on these AI-accessible skills.

Humans Excel at Higher-Order Thinking

Analyze: Finding relationships (requires human judgment)

Evaluate: Making judgments based on values and evidence

Create: Generating original ideas tied to personal experience

The Opportunity: Focus on creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving.

The Balanced Approach

Let AI handle: Information delivery, fact checking, data analysis

Focus humans on: Creative synthesis, meaning-making, ethical reasoning, collaborative problem-solving

Result: Students develop valuable skills while AI becomes a learning tool.

What is XR AI?

XR AI creates and powers immersive experiences. Navigate through key capabilities:

🌐 Immersive Worlds

AI generates interactive 3D environments. Students could explore ancient Rome with AI-powered characters that respond intelligently. The environment adapts based on student choices.

🤖 Intelligent NPCs & Adaptive Content

AI-powered characters respond naturally to student interactions — serving as tutors, mentors, or partners. Content difficulty adjusts based on performance in real-time.

🎯 Customized Learning

Each student receives a personalized experience tailored to their pace, style, and goals. One student explores at beginner level while another tackles advanced challenges.

Spatial AI vs XR AI: Key Differences

Both are important, but they solve different problems:

Understanding the Physical World

What it does: Perceives, interprets, and models the physical world in real-time.

  • Recognizes objects and surfaces
  • Measures depth and distance
  • Tracks motion and movement
  • Enables navigation and spatial awareness

Powers: Robotics, autonomous vehicles, smart glasses

Powering Immersive Experiences

What it does: Creates, manages, and personalizes immersive environments.

  • Generates 3D content and worlds
  • Controls intelligent NPCs and avatars
  • Adapts content to user performance
  • Personalizes learning experiences

Powers: VR training, AR games, intelligent tutoring
